About COVID-19 HELP

As an online marketplace for local services, at PinDoit.com, we have altered our platform to help people in need during this unique time. Our goal is to connect our local neighbors in need – the elderly, disabled, or immune-compromised – with voluteers who can help with errands and services. We have added a COVID-19 HELP category to our website. There are NO FEES for any COVID-19 HELP task.Together we will overcome the challenges posed by the corona virus. Be smart, be safe and thank you for your willingness to support your community. Please check PinDoit.com often for updates and new Pin requests for help.

How It Works

Local residents looking for free help should log into PinDoit.com and create a Pinner profile. Volunteers sign up by creating a Doer profile. COVID-19 HELP is listed among the main service categories such as “Home Services” and “Pet Care”. Those in need should create a Pin that details the desired service, Volunteers sign up and select the type of service that they can provide.

Current COVID-19 categories include:

  • Shopping
  • Food Preparation
  • Pet Care
  • Errands
  • Meals
  • Outdoor Services
  • Driving
  • Babysitting
  • Other

Instructions for those in need

Register as a person in need (Pinner)

  1. Go to www.PinDoit.com and click register.
  2. Select Pinner when registering.
  3. Complete the following sections in Account Settings:
    1. Profile
    2. Contact Info
    3. Location Settings (so we can match you with the Volunteers closest to you)
    4. Remaining sections are optional for COVID-19 HELP. You do NOT have to complete credit card information since the service is FREE.
    5. If you would like to reimburse the Volunteer (Doer) electronically for goods (groceries, food, etc.) set up your bank account in Bank Information (not required).

Pin a Request

To submit a request for help you will need to create a “Pin”.

  1. Click on the Pin icon in the upper right-hand corner of any page or click Pin a Job in one of the green circles on the Home Page.
  2. Select the COVID-19 HELP category and then a subcategory for the type of assistance needed.
  3. Add specific information to the Pin for the volunteer such as:
    • What specific services or items are needed.
    • When you need this service done or items delivered.
    • Where you would like items delivered or a service performed.
    • Why you need help. Please be candid and help volunteers understand your concerns and why you cannot leave your home. (e.g. I’m disabled and need items placed at my door and not the bottom of my steps).
    • How you are going to pay for items; your preferred method of contact, etc.
  4. When your Pin information is complete, Post it to Public so anyone on the site can see it and respond to you. Don’t worry, you will remain anonymous.
  5. You can choose to search registered volunteers (Doers that match your criteria) and invite them to respond.

Look for the COVID-19 HELP badge that designates volunteers (Doers) willing to help.

Choose a Volunteer

  1. When a volunteer (Doer) sees your Pin and offers to help, you will be notified by email. The email subject will read, “PinDo: You’ve received a quote”.
  2. Select the link in the email to go to your Pin and select HIRE.
  3. When the task is complete, please close the Pin and review your volunteer (Doer).

Instructions for volunteers

Register as a volunteer (Doer)

  1. Go to www.PinDoit.com and click register.
  2. Select Doer when registering and then either Individual or Company.
  3. Complete the following sections in Account Settings:
    1. Profile
    2. Contact Info
    3. Location Settings (so we can match you with the Pinners closest to you)
    4. Services Offered. Be sure to select COVID-19 HELP and choose subcategories.
    5. Remaing sections are optional for COVID-19 HELP. You do NOT have to complete credit card information since the service is FREE.
    6. If your services include shopping for goods (groceries, food, etc.) you can set up your bank account in Bank Information but this is not required.

Watch tutorial video for Doer signup

Check out existing Public Pins

  1. Click Find a Pin from the main navigation menu.
  2. Search the listed Pins. They are pre-filtered for your area and the subcategories that you selected when registering.
  3. Click View Details or Apply Now to respond to a Pin request.

Respond to a Pin (help request)

Once you have completed your profile settings Pinners in need may find you directly.

  1. If you receive a direct request it will come via email.
  2. Select QUOTE to volunteer for the Pin; or REJECT if you cannot perform the requested task.
  3. If you select QUOTE, write a short message to the Pinner in the Cover Letter section. Then click SAVE at the bottom of the quote page.

Get Hired!

If a Pinner selects you for help they will accept your quote and you will receive an email informing you that you are hired! Please make sure to respond right away.

  1. Complete the task.
  2. Select REQUEST PAYMENT – there is NO PAYMENT of course but our platform was built for monetary transactions and we are doing our best to modify it for COVID-19 HELP. By selecting REQUEST PAYMENT, it tells the Pinner that the task is complete and that you are ready to close the Pin.
  3. The Pin will close and the Pinner will leave a review.

Volunteer Guidelines

Volunteers should be at low risk of contracting the virus and are asked to follow CDC guidelines, taking ALL necessary precautions to protect themselves and others by frequently washing hands, wiping down containers and adhering to social distancing recommendations. Pinners should provide detailed instructions such as where to leave items for drop off or what indentification may be required for pick up of prescriptions.
