PinDo CREW Explained

PinDo CREW members are advocates of the PinDo online services marketplace. CREW members are tasked to help strengthen their local economies by spreading awareness of the PinDo platform and commerce opportunities. The formula is simple: cultivate, initiate, accumulate.

  • 1

    Cultivate

    Spread the word, make people aware of PinDo, educate service providers (Doers) and consumers (Pinners) about the opportunity to make connections and find service providers and customers in their local community.

  • 2

    Initiate

    Sign up Doers to the platform and help them reach new customers.

  • 3

    Accumulate

    Make money. Create a new revenue stream that once set up, pays you for 5 years!

How Does the Program Work

  • 1. Apply

    Fill out the form by selecting "APPLY" below. The process is simple but we need an application to help us help you succeed.

  • 2. Accept

    Your application will be reviewed at PinDo. We review your submitted information and check the CREW opportunity available in your local community. If we are actively expanding in your area, we will return an invite to become a CREW member.

  • 3. Sign up Doers

    This is your opportunity to earn your 25%. Educate prospective users and get them familiar with the platform.

  • 4. Get Paid

    Once they use PinDo, the benefits will become clear and you will have created a new revenue stream for yourself.
